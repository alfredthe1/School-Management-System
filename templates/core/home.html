About Happy Child School

Located in the vibrant town of Arua in Uganda's West Nile Region, Happy Child Nursery and Primary School has been a beacon of quality education since 2010.

We provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth for every child.

Our Commitment
  • Child-centered learning
  • Strong moral and religious values
  • Modern facilities and qualified staff
  • Focus on Ugandan curriculum + life skills

Our Programs

From early childhood to primary education, tailored for the children of Arua and West Nile.

Nursery Section

Play-based learning for ages 3-6. Focus on social skills, early literacy, numeracy, and creative play in a caring environment.

Primary Section (P1–P7)

Comprehensive Ugandan curriculum with strong emphasis on Mathematics, English, Science, Social Studies, and co-curricular activities.

Co-curricular & Clubs

Sports, Music, Drama, Debate, Agriculture, and ICT clubs to develop well-rounded, confident children.
